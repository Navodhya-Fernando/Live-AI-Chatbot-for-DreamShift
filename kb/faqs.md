**Yes.** We:

### Will you design visuals for LinkedIn?
**Yes.** We create:

Further updates become a **paid revamp**. Pricing depends on the scope—WhatsApp us for a quick quote.

- Apply to jobs during the 60-day window using our documents
- Provide proof (screenshots, confirmations, or an application log)

Approved refunds are processed within **5–7 business days** to your original payment method.

### What’s not refundable?
- Completed/approved services **outside** the 60-day window
- Lack of job applications within the policy period
- **Express** fees
- Requests beyond original scope
- Projects without settled balances
