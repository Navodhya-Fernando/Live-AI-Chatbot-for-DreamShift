# DreamShift Services Overview

DreamShift provides professional career branding services designed to help job seekers stand out in competitive job markets. Our services combine strategic positioning, modern document design, ATS optimization, and customized career messaging.

Each service can be purchased individually or included within one of our packages.

---

## Resume / CV Writing

A professionally written Resume / CV optimized for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).  
DreamShift focuses on:

- Clear career positioning
- Modern, recruiter-friendly formatting
- Industry-specific ATS keywords
- Achievement-based bullet points
- Results-focused responsibility statements
- Role-specific alignment

Resumes are written in **1–2 pages**, depending on career level.  
All resumes include **ATS Keyword Research** and **modern templates**.

DreamShift offers a **60-Day Job Interview Guarantee** on selected packages.

---

## Cover Letter Writing

A customized cover letter written to complement your resume.  
Includes:

- Targeted messaging for the role
- Company-aligned value statements
- Professional tone and structure
- Action-oriented closing calls

In the **Ultimate Package**, cover letters are **targeted** and **custom-crafted** for specific roles.  
All clients receive access to the **DreamShift Cover Letter Toolkit** after the project to help them tailor future letters independently.

---

## LinkedIn Optimization

A professionally optimized LinkedIn profile designed to improve employer visibility.  
This service covers:

- Headline rewriting
- About section rewriting
- Experience section rewriting
- Keyword optimization
- Recruiter search visibility improvements

Branding elements such as **LinkedIn Banner Designs** are included in higher packages.

## ATS Keyword Research

All resumes are optimized against Applicant Tracking Systems.  
This includes:

- Identification of relevant job keywords
- Incorporation of industry terminology
- Alignment with job descriptions
- Recruiter search phrasing

This improves the chances of passing automated screening filters.

This session is used to:

- Understand your background, goals, and challenges
- Clarify career direction
- Identify key strengths to highlight
- Recommend the best solution based on your needs

Consultations are used to ensure **precision and personalization**.
